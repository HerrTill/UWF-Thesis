The purpose of this paper is to perform a statistical analysis on students' academic performance data, from the $2020-2021$ academic school year to uncover if they were affected by the restrictions set out by local and federal governments in the state of Florida during the COVID-19 pandemic. The government restrictions forced students and teachers to learn and teach online, respectively, without being given proper training or resources. This analysis will seek to uncover the intricacies of learning through the pandemic and reveal the overall effects on students' academic progress.
